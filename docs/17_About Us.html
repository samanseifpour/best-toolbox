19. About Us¶

BEST Toolbox is an open source project aiming to increase objectivity, reliability and reproducibility in non invasive brain stimulation research. It was created in December 2018, with a focus on closed-loop real-time brain state dependent brain stimulation experiment control, now extending its features to the various modalities of neuromodulation. BEST Toolbox is developed in the open on GitHub.
